Privacy Policy for Focus25

  Last Updated: December 24, 2025

  Overview

  Focus25 is a Pomodoro timer Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

  Data Collection

  Focus25 does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

  Data Storage

  Focus25 stores the following data locally on your device only:

  | Data                    | Purpose                                                  | Location             |
  |-------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|----------------------|
  | Timer settings          | Remember your preferences (focus duration, break length) | Chrome local storage |
  | Timer state             | Resume timer after browser restart                       | Chrome local storage |
  | Notification preference | Enable/disable alerts                                    | Chrome sync storage  |

  Third-Party Services

  Focus25 does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

  Data Sharing

  We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

  Permissions Used

  | Permission    | Why Needed                  |
  |---------------|-----------------------------|
  | storage       | Save your settings locally  |
  | alarms        | Run the countdown timer     |
  | notifications | Alert you when sessions end |

  Your Rights

  Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can clear all data by:
  1. Right-clicking the extension icon
  2. Selecting "Remove from Chrome"

  Or clear storage via Chrome DevTools.

  Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

  Contact

  If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
